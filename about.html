I'm a Gen AI engineer from Pune with a deep obsession for making language models do useful things in the real world.

My journey started with classical ML — decision trees, SVMs, the classics — but I pivoted hard into NLP and LLMs when GPT-3 dropped. Since then I've been building end-to-end AI systems: from chunking strategies and embedding models all the way to production-ready APIs and agent workflows.

I believe the most interesting work in AI right now is at the intersection of reliable systems and creative applications. Getting LLMs to consistently do something useful is a much harder engineering problem than it looks.

When I'm not shipping AI projects, I write about LLM architecture on my blog, contribute to open-source ML tools, and mentor junior devs getting into the space.
